Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 05, 2026

Vignali Ventures ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information:

  • Persistent Identifier: We may collect a persistent device or account identifier, such as a Firebase User ID (UID) or a locally-generated device ID. This is used solely to save your game progress, manage in-game purchases, and maintain your account across sessions.
  • We do not collect any other personal data, including your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or any other identifiable information.
  • We do not use any tracking or analytics tools (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or third-party tracking SDKs).

2. In-App Purchases

Our App allows users to purchase in-game currency (Sparks). These purchases are processed securely through the Google Play Store (and Apple App Store in the future). We do not directly handle your payment information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected identifier only for:

  • Saving and restoring your game progress
  • Processing and validating in-app purchases
  • Providing customer support

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With Google (for Google Play services and payments)
  • As required by law

5. Your Rights

For California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion of your data, and opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@vignalventures.com.

For European Union / GDPR Residents

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability regarding your personal data. You also have the right to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact support@vignalventures.com.

California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA)

California residents may request information about how their information is shared with third parties for direct marketing purposes. We do not share your information in this manner.

6. Data Retention

We retain your identifier only as long as necessary to provide the game service and fulfill legal obligations.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@vignalventures.com
